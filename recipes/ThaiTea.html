Thai Tea

Ingredients

  • Thai Tea
  • Half and Half
  • Sugar
  • Water

Directions

  1. Boil 4 cups of water
  2. Add 1 cup of tea and 1 cup of sugar
  3. Take of the heat and allow tea to steep for 30 minutes.
  4. Strain tea into another container.
  5. Add ice, tea and half and half to a cup.
  6. Enjoy.
