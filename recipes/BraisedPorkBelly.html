Braised Pork Belly

Braised pork belly served on a bed of steamed rice

Ingredients

  • Pork Belly
  • Eggs
  • Oyster Sauce
  • Fish Sauce
  • Garlic
  • Coconut Soda
  • Sugar

Directions

  1. Cut pork belly into 1 inch chunks
  2. Boil the eggs, peel and set aside
  3. In a medium sized pot, brown the pork belly with minced garlic
  4. Add the oyster sauce, fish sauce, and coconut soda
  5. Bring pot to a boil and skim off any foam
  6. Add eggs to pot
  7. In a small pan, caramlize some sugar
  8. Add the caramalized sugar to the pot and stir
  9. Simmer until meat is tender and serve on top of rice
